Introduction

简介

World Class Education

世界级一流教育

 

The British School of Nanjing offers a modern and vibrant education to boys and girls in Early Years, Primary and Secondary School. The school serves expatriate families looking to provide their children with a private education of the highest standard. We nurture high aspirations for each and every one of our students. Many achieve great academic results while others discover a passion or special talent. Our day-to-day focus is on helping pupils to thrive in their own unique way.

 

南京英国学校旨在为全世界的幼儿、小学及中学生提供现代化并充满活力的教育。学校服务于希望子女得到一流私立教育的外籍家庭并致力于培养每一名学生志存高远。我们培养很多学生取得了优异的成绩，而更多的人发现了自身的天赋和才能。这都要归功于我们日常关注的重点——帮助每一名学生以自己独特的方式茁壮成长。

 

We offer a rich and varied curriculum based on the English National Curriculum that allows pupils to explore the breadth and depth of their abilities. Work is underpinned by a highly qualified and dedicated teaching body. Small class sizes and low student to teacher ratios mean that pupils can receive the support they require to make good progress both academically and in their personal development.

 

我们提供以英式教育为基础，丰富多样的课程，让学生去探索自身能力的广度和深度。高素质的专业教师队伍、小班制和学生与教师的恰当比例都保证了学生可以最大程度上获得学业及个人全方面发展的支持。

 

The school enjoys a vibrant day-to-day life with children participating in Music, Drama, Arts, and Sports programs as well as a number of other Enrichment activities.

 

孩子们可以在学校通过参加音乐，艺术，戏剧表演，体育运动及其他各种丰富的活动来度过充实而有趣的一天。

 

BSN has preserved a strong sense of community since its foundation and every member of the school has many opportunities to contribute to the welfare of the school community. We are proud of the role our families play in our school and in turn the role we play in their lives during their time in Nanjing.

 

自成立以来，南京英国学校一直保持着强烈的集体主义精神，每一位在校师生都有很多机会为学校这个大家庭作出自己的贡献；我们为那些成为学校一员的家庭感到自豪，也为我们能在众多家庭的南京生活时光中添上一笔而感到荣幸。

 

BSN is part of the British Schools Foundation, a network of 11 leading schools in America, Europe and Asia.

 

南京英国学校是英国学校集团旗下的一所学校，我们在北美、欧洲和亚洲共有11所学校。
